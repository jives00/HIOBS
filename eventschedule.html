50th Anniversary Bash

The festivities will kick-off with a cocktail reception and benefit dinner at Point Lookout Resort in Lincolnville, Maine, about 8 miles north of Camden along Rte. 1. With a magnificent view of the ocean, join us as the sun sets to celebrate five decades of the people and programs of HIOBS!

Reunion on Hurricane Island
Saturday, August 23 to Sunday, August 24
Hurricane Island, Maine

The celebration will continue on Saturday when we head to Hurricane Island for a weekend reunion that will include a 50th Annual General Meeting and Lobster Bake. If you can stay for the night, we’ll have a Run & Dip and Morning Meeting on Sunday before heading back to the mainland. So, dig out your tent, sleeping bag and headlamp for a rustic camping experience. Join us as we revel in our history and imagine the next 50 years of HIOBS.

Our island host for the weekend will be the Hurricane Island Foundation's Center for Science and Leadership,
a non-profit educational organization operating on Hurricane Island.

Please register early. Capacity on Hurricane Island limited to 300 persons.
