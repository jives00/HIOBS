Camping at Hurricane Island
Designated sites will be identified upon your arrival to Hurricane and volunteers will be on hand to help set up camp for the night. Please bring a headlamp or flashlight along with your tent and sleeping bag. 

Cabins on Hurricane Island
 There will be limited cabin space available for an additional fee to the Hurricane Island Foundation. If you’d like to check on availability, please contact Stefanie Mojonnier at (828) 239-2403 or smojonnier@hiobs.org.  We highly recommend that you register now to ensure your spot in the celebration and then add on a cabin if available.

Transportation to and from Hurricane Island

Our goal is to get everyone out to and back from Hurricane as safely and as quickly as possible!  As you register (on this website or via your invitation reply card), please share your transportation plans for the trip to Hurricane Island so we can best plan for your arrival. While we hope you can spend Saturday night, we have a limited capacity of 300 for the weekend and an overnight capacity of 150 people. Whether you plan to arrive by ferry or by private vessel, you are required to register in advance. You will be asked to check-in with HIOBS staff upon your arrival Saturday August 23, either at the Rockland Ferry terminal or on Hurricane Island.

Maine State Ferry Service: Vinalhaven Ferry

    • The Maine State Ferry travels from Rockland to Vinalhaven daily, several times during the day.

    • Please plan to catch the 7:00, 8:45, or 10:30am ferry out of Rockland to arrive on Vinalhaven with plenty of time to take one of the motor vessel shuttles to Hurricane Island by 1:00 PM for the Annual General Meeting. Shuttle runs will start from Vinalhaven around 8:20am, the expected arrival time of the first ferry from Rockland that morning. Volunteers will be on the dock to direct you to the party!  

    • Round-trip transportation to and from Hurricane Island is included as part of your weekend reunion registration. If you are taking the ferry, you will receive a ferry ticket as you check-in with HIOBS staff at the Rockland ferry terminal on Saturday, August 23.

    • Transportation plans are subject to change, so please include an email and phone number with your registration so we can notify you of any and all important updates in a timely manner.

Private Vessel
You are welcome to bring your own vessel out to Hurricane, however please keep in mind that moorings will not be available. Bring your own hook! Guests arriving by private vessel will be asked to check in at Hurricane's main pier upon arrival.
