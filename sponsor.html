Sponsorship Opportunities
Friday, August 22, 2014

On Friday evening, the Hurricane Island Outward Bound School will celebrate 50 years at its Anniversary Bash, a benefit dinner at Point Lookout Resort in Lincolnville, Maine. The evening’s events will include a tribute to HIOBS founder, Peter O. Willauer, and an auction, featuring the work of some of Maine’s finest artists as well as selected HIOBS memorabilia.

Becoming a sponsor of the 50th Anniversary Bash guarantees you a reservation to this special anniversary event and will provide vital support to future Hurricane Island Outward Bound School programs. Proceeds from the Bash will help us continue our mission of changing lives through challenge and discovery. Your gift will enable us to continue putting deserving students on life-changing wilderness expeditions.
