Auction

Thirty-seven distinguished painters and photographers with ties to Maine and Outward Bound are contributing extraordinary works of art to be auctioned at HIOBS' 50th Anniversary Bash, a benefit dinner Friday, August 22 at Point Lookout in Lincolnville, Maine. Proceeds from art reflecting our special program areas will support the continued work of the Hurricane Island Outward Bound School. Proceeds from some of the work auctioned will also benefit the Hurricane Island Foundation's Center for Science and Leadership.**
