About the Auction
Forty-nine pieces of art created by thirty-nine notable artists with ties to Maine and Hurricane Island are offered for auction this Friday night. Hurricane Island was the first program base site of the Hurricane Island Outward Bound School and is currently home to the Hurricane Island Foundation Center for Science and Leadership.

50th Anniversary Art + Auction Invitational
On August 9, twenty-one of the contributing artists visited Hurricane Island and worked en plein air, creating twenty-seven pieces for auction. Proceeds from artwork created that day will be shared between the Hurricane Island Outward Bound School and the Hurricane Island Foundation Center for Science and Leadership to support the mission and ongoing programs of each organization.Â
